About Me

I am Legion Cunningham, a software development student who enjoys creating dark-themed websites, learning programming logic, and building practical digital projects.

I matriculated in 2025 and I am currently studying Software Development at UniCollegeZA. My main interests are web development, problem solving, and improving my technical skills through real projects.

I enjoy building responsive interfaces with HTML, CSS, and Tailwind CSS. I also work on Scratch projects to strengthen my programming logic and creativity.

Beyond coding, I like designing organised Discord systems such as ticket panels, rule embeds, documentation layouts, and structured server information channels.
